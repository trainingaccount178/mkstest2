About Event

The Maker's Carnival is a two-day event hosted by JUMakerspace, the innovation cell at JECRC University. About us

About
JECRC University

JECRC University in Jaipur, Rajasthan offers Bachelor's and Master's degree education to students across the globe. It is driven by the spirit of innovation-lead research. JU aims at creating valuable resources for the creation of research and innovative culture, academic and professional enhancement and cultural enrichment.

About
JU Makerspace

JU Makerspace is North India’s First Student Driven collaborative Workspace packed with equipment and tools where students with a bend for invention and innovation can come together under one roof and turn their ideas into reality by building, designing, creating, and engineering new things, products, etc. for bringing a change and to help society. Here we work on technologies like 3D Printing, Robotics, Mechatronics, Electronics, CAD Modelling, AR/VR. We have also introduced the trending Buzz i.e Blockchain and Cryptography in Makerspace.
